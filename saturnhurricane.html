Unveiling Nature's Fury



Saturn's South Pole Storm

A hurricane-like storm located at the South Pole, featuring a distinct eye-wall. NASA reported that Cassini observed this storm in November 2006, and apart from Earth, such an eye-wall cloud has not been observed on any other planet.

Another Massive Hurricane in the North

In September 2013, Cassini also discovered a massive hurricane in the north polar region of Saturn.
