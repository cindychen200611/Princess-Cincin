Unlocking Saturn's Secrets:

The Enigmatic Hexagon Phenomenon



The Saturn Hexagon

The Saturn Hexagon was first discovered during the mission of the Voyager spacecraft, and notably, this hexagonal cloud structure is unique to Saturn's north pole.

Color Shift Over Time

According to observations from the Cassini spacecraft between 2012 and 2016, the color of Saturn's Hexagon shifted from predominantly blue to mostly golden. In other words, the Saturn Hexagon we see now is quite different from its original appearance. It might be a new type of aurora, certainly worth a closer look.
