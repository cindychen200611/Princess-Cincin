Unveiling Mysteries Since 1974



Journey into the Enchanting World of Caloris Basin: A Geological Marvel on Mercury

Step into the awe-inspiring realm of Caloris Basin, a monumental discovery dating back to 1974. This colossal impact crater, surrounded by towering walls reaching up to 2 kilometers high, cradles a mesmerizing lava plain reminiscent of lunar seas. At its heart lies the enigmatic Apollo Dolos crater, spanning 40 kilometers, and surrounding it, a landscape adorned with intricate radial grooves and extended faults. This mysterious terrain, known as Pantheon Fossae, defies easy explanation, adding to the allure of Caloris Basin. Delve into the wonders of this extraordinary area, where science and wonder harmonize, creating the fascinating tapestry of the Caloris Group.
