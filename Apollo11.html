Apollo 11:

Relive the Moon Landing



The Historic Moon Landing That Inspired Millions

Apollo 11 launched from Cape Kennedy on July 16, 1969. An estimated 650 million people watched Armstrong’s televised image and heard his voice describe the event as he took “…one small step for a man, one giant leap for mankind” on July 20, 1969. Mare Tranquillitatis was where the first ever moon landing approximately took place. By visiting the site, you can relive the emotions or even find the memorabilia left on the Moon back then!
