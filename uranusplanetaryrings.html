Uranus' Rings:

Where Beauty Meets Rarity



Uranus' Unique Ring System

It is the second ring system discovered in the solar system after Saturn's. Currently, there are 13 known rings around Uranus, and all but two are extremely narrow - typically only a few kilometers wide.

Diverse Colors in Uranus' Rings

In April 2006, images of the new rings released by the Keck Observatory revealed one ring to be blue and another to be red. Not only is it a rare ring system in the solar system, but it also displays different colors, making it truly worth admiring.
