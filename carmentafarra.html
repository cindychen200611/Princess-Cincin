Unusual Venusian Lava Domes

A pancake dome (farra) is an unusual type of lava dome found on the planet Venus. They are usually between 10 and 100 times larger than volcanic domes formed on Earth.

Broad, Flat Profile

Pancake domes have a broad, flat profile and are thought to form from one large, slow eruption of viscous silica-rich lava.

Central Bowl-like Feature

They usually have a central bowl-like feature, but it is thought that these pits form after the eruption as the lava cools and emits gas rather than being a vent from which the lava originated.
