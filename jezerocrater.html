Jezero Crater:

Martian Mystique Unveiled



Perseverance Rover's Geologic Discovery

The Perseverance rover has captured new images that appear to reveal geologic evidence of a fast-flowing river that fed into Jezero Crater. Perseverance began exploring the remnants of the environment within the crater after landing on the red planet in February 2021. The rover discovered a fan-shaped deposit in the area that stands 820 feet (250 meters) tall, likely the remnants of an ancient river delta, in the crater.

Diverse Water-Associated Environments

With the rover Perseverance, we’re actually moving through different environments that once had water associated with them. So here in Jezero, we have evidence of lakes, deltas and ancient rivers.
