Mare Orientale:

Moon's Hidden Gem



The Lunar Bullseye Hidden on the Far Side of the Moon

Mare Orientale is a lunar mare. It is located on the western border of the near side and far side of the Moon, and is difficult to see from an Earthbound perspective. Images from spacecraft have revealed it to be one of the most striking large scale lunar features, resembling a target ring bullseye. Space travel is the only chance to witness the wonder with your own eyes!
