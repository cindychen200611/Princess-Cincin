Baltis Vallis:

Venusian Majesty Beckons



Basic Info

➼coordinates: 37.30N 161.40E
➼length: 6800 kilometers
➼width: 1~3 kilometers

River-Like Appearance

Venusian channels (vallis) resemble terrestrial rivers.

Volcanic Origins

They also show characteristics of flow, leading to the conclusion that they are likely formed from volcanic flows. They formed in very short timescales (1~100 years) , indicating very fast movement and erosion of lavas.

Extraordinary Length

Baltis Vallis is the longest known channel in the Solar System, even longer than the Nile!
